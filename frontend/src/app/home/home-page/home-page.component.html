GMU Tutors Web Application (name pending)

What is this for?

  • The idea of this application is to connect GMU students to reliable peer mentors and tutors.
  • It was originally an idea for an Amazon Alexa skill.

How do I use it?

  • Just click on "Find Tutors" to browse through available tutors.
  • You can click on "Campus Map" to see which tutors are currently on campus!

How can I help?

  • As a student, you can help by rating the tutors that you signed up for.
  • You can also sign up to be a tutor yourself!
