What is this for?

This is a web application for GMU students to connect with peer mentors more effectively.


You can read more about the inspiration and motivation of the project our GitHub page.

What does it do?

As a student: You can search for tutors for different subjects and see their availability/price/location.

As a tutor: You can market yourself on the website to expand your reach to more students than posting fliers on campus.


Why should I use it?

You'll be connected with all of the other students that use the site. This will only work as a community effort.
